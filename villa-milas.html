A Charming Hotel in Historic Mostar

Hotel Villa Milas offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and traditional Bosnian warmth. Located just 250m from Mostar's city center and only a 5-minute walk from the iconic Old Bridge, our hotel provides an ideal base for exploring this UNESCO World Heritage city.

With 10 elegantly appointed rooms, personalized service, and a convenient location, Villa Milas is the perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers seeking an authentic Mostar experience.

Discover Mostar

Hotel Villa Milas is perfectly positioned in the historic center of Mostar, just a short walk from the famous Old Bridge (Stari Most) and the charming Old Town with its traditional bazaars and Ottoman architecture.

Explore the rich culture and history of Bosnia and Herzegovina from our convenient location, with easy access to restaurants, cafes, and attractions.
